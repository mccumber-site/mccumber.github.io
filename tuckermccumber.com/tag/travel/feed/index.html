She….is absolutely perfect and this vacation has not only been perfect for me, but perfect for such a well deserving STRONG woman like her.

Someday, somehow, I will repay EVERYONE for what you’ve all done (and continue to do) for me and Ky. I can’t even begin to thank you all and my words will never be enough. I will never forget this for as long as I live. Thank you again and again all…you have given me such a huge smile that can never be taken away
